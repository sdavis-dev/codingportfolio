About Me

Hi, I’m SeDerrick, a passionate Full-Stack Developer working towards becoming a Software Engineer. My journey with computers started in childhood, and by high school, I found my passion for coding through hands-on courses at my local CTE center.
I specialize in web and full-stack development, with proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Java, SQL, and more. I love bringing ideas to life through clean, functional code. My portfolio includes Python projects that showcase my problem-solving skills and a Java/Android Studio app that I'm building to gain deeper experience in mobile development.
Beyond coding, I’m particularly excited about web applications, mobile development, and AI. I thrive on solving complex problems and pushing my skills further with every project.
When I’m not coding, I’m likely exploring new tech trends, working on personal projects, or brainstorming my next big idea. Feel free to explore my work and reach out—I’d love to collaborate!
